Day 1;*What is data and analysis

Data and analysis. data is simply facts and numbers used to analyze something or make decisions while analysis is the process of breaking a complex topic or substance into smaller parts in order to gain a better understanding of it

*What is data analysis, data analytics, data scientist and business analyst?

Data analysis is the process of cleaning, changing and processing raw data and extracting relevant or useful information that helps businesses make informed decisions. it is based on past information or events. Data analytics is the examining data sets in order to find trend and draw conclusions about the information they contain. it is also based on future information or events. Data scientist is an analytics professional who is responsible for collecting, analyzing and interpreting data to help drive decision making in an organization. They also work with large amount of data and handle mathematistical, statistical computer programming. Business analyst are agents of change; professionals who analyze business or organisation by documenting its system and processes, assessing its business model, identifying vulnerabilities and devising solutions. They straddle the line between IT and the business to help bridge the gap and improve efficiency.

*What is database?

Database is information that is setup for easy access, management and updating. Computer databases typicallly stores aggregations of data records or files that contain information, such as sales transactions and product information.

*Database management system (DBMS)

Database management system is a system that gives facilities to perform several kinds of operations on such a system for either manipulation of data in the database or the management of the database structure itself.
